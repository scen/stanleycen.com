hikes.

trip reports from my backpacking & camping adventures

August 18, 2017 — August 20, 2017

Goat Rocks Wilderness: Packwood Lake to Old Snowy Mountain Loop

An extraordinary trip that included summitting the highest point along the PCT in Washington, grand alpine vistas, two ridge walks, lush forests, two high camps, and two sub-alpine lakes. Three passing NOBO PCT thru-hikers mentioned that this was the most epic and beautiful section they've experienced. I can see why.

July 29, 2017 — July 30, 2017

Desolation Wilderness: Lake Aloha

The most popular destination in the Desolation Wilderness, and for good reason. A beautiful overnighter to a spectacular alpine lake flanked by the Crystal Range.

July 23, 2017

Yosemite National Park: Clouds Rest

My favorite hike to date. An epic hike with a spectacular payoff: unforgettable 360 degree views of Yosemite Valley, Tenaya Canyon and the park's backcountry.

July 22, 2017 + July 04, 2017 + June 11, 2017

Yosemite National Park: Vernal & Nevada Falls (2017)

This hike is so awesome, I did it three times this summer. Which adds up to six times in my life. I think that says a lot.

July 15, 2017

Pacific Crest Trail: CA Section K near Donner Summit

A short jaunt to a few peaks overlooking Donner Lake and the city of Truckee, CA. Bonus: there's a wooden rope swing on a tree near the end!
